Thank you for installing {{ .Chart.Name }}.

Your Elasticsearch cluster has been deployed!

To check the status of your cluster:

  kubectl get pods -l app={{ .Values.clusterName }}

To check the services:

  kubectl get svc -l app={{ .Values.clusterName }}

To access Elasticsearch:

  # Port-forward to access locally
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ .Values.clusterName }}-{{ .Values.nodeGroup }} 9200:9200

  # Then access at: http://localhost:9200

To check cluster health:

  # After port-forwarding
  curl http://localhost:9200/_cluster/health?pretty

To scale your cluster (production):

  helm upgrade {{ .Release.Name }} . --set replicas=3
